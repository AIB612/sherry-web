## Overview

Led the design of TCL smart home application for iOS and Android, increasing new users by 20%.

## Challenge

TCL needed a unified mobile app to control their growing ecosystem of smart home devices — from kitchen appliances to lighting and security.

## Solution

- Designed intuitive device control interfaces
- Created a consistent design language across iOS and Android
- Implemented data-driven product roadmaps
- Integrated R&D for electrical kitchen appliances into the digital strategy

## Results

- **20%** increase in new users
- Unified smart home experience across 50+ device types
- 4.5★ average app store rating
