## Overview

Swiss EV charging subsidy explorer with interactive map and ROI calculator.

## Challenge

Swiss cantons offer different EV charging subsidies. Homeowners and businesses need a clear way to understand what's available in their region.

## Solution

- Interactive 2D SVG Swiss canton map
- 9 cantons with subsidy data (ZH, BE, GE, VD, NE, BS, TI, TG, GR)
- Trilingual support (DE/FR/EN)
- Dynamic ROI calculator
- Postal code search
- Glassmorphism UI design

## Results

- Business-ready MVP completed
- Basel-Stadt as primary market
- Conversion CTA for lead generation
